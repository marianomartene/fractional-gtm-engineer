Not a strategist who hands you a deck. Not an agency you wait three months on.

first working fix into your live stack, then runs and expands it.

## The 60-second Process-Market Fit diagnostic

Tick every box that's true.

**Pipeline**
- [ ] Most deals still come from the founder's network or referrals
- [ ] "More on LinkedIn" is the current plan for net-new pipeline
- [ ] No repeatable way to put right-fit accounts in front of you

**Conversion**
- [ ] Interest shows up, then dies (demos, trials, replies stall)
- [ ] Every deal rides on the founder being in the room
- [ ] No written "why we win"

**Data and system**
- [ ] You don't fully trust your own CRM numbers
- [ ] More tools than last year, same confusion
- [ ] If you took two weeks off, growth would basically stop

```
   0-2 boxes   you're fine. come back when it stops scaling.
   3-5 boxes   you have the Process-Market Fit gap. one lever is leaking.
   6+  boxes   it's all in your head and it's the bottleneck. fix the system.
```
