# Awesome Fractional GTM [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated guide to fractional go-to-market: the operators, tools, playbooks, and
> frameworks behind hiring a part-time GTM leader or GTM engineer instead of a full-time team.

Fractional GTM is how early-stage and lean B2B companies get senior go-to-market
firepower without a full-time hire: a part-time operator or GTM engineer who builds
the pipeline system, then hands it off or keeps running it. This list maps the space:
what the role is, who does it (peers and competitors included, listed fairly), the
tooling, and the playbooks worth reading. Every entry below is a real, verifiable
page. Nothing here is invented.

Maintained by [Mariano Martene](https://marianomartene.com), a fractional GTM engineer.
Contributions welcome, see [Contributing](#contributing).

## What is Fractional GTM

A fractional GTM hire, whether titled GTM engineer, fractional Head of Growth, or
fractional RevOps leader, works part-time or on a fixed engagement instead of full-time.
The pitch: senior go-to-market capability, embedded in the live stack, without the
cost and ramp time of a full-time hire.

- [GTM Engineering: What It Is and How to Hire in 2026](https://www.clay.com/blog/gtm-engineering) - Clay's own definition of the role, from the company that coined the term in 2023.
- [What's Behind the Rise in Roles Like GTM Engineer, RevOps Engineer and GTM Ops Engineer?](https://martech.org/gtm-engineer-revops-engineer-gtm-ops-engineer/) - independent trade-press look (MarTech) at why these titles appeared.
- [GTM Glossary](https://tools.marianomartene.com/glossary/) - a plain-English glossary of go-to-market engineering terms (GTM engineer, Process-Market Fit, signal-based outbound, ICP, and more), maintained alongside this list.

## Tools & Automation

Outbound sequencers, CRM, workflow automation, and the growing pile of Claude
Code / AI-agent skill packs built for GTM work. Competitor repos included on
purpose: an honest list is more useful than a flattering one.

- [agent-gtm-skills](https://github.com/chadboyda/agent-gtm-skills) - 18 AI agent skills that turn a coding agent into a GTM operator, covering positioning, pricing, outbound, inbound, and retention.
- [awesome-gtm-engineering](https://github.com/marketinguys/awesome-gtm-engineering) - the other awesome-list in this space. Strong on data infrastructure, attribution, and analytics tooling; as of this writing it has no entries on fractional GTM hiring or practitioners, which is part of why this list exists.
- [gtm-agents](https://github.com/gtmagents/gtm-agents) - a large (67-plugin) production collection of GTM agents and skills for Claude Code, covering sales, marketing, customer success, and RevOps.
- [gtm-eng-skills](https://github.com/getaero-io/gtm-eng-skills) - Claude Code skills for waterfall email enrichment, TAM building, signal discovery, and job-change detection, built on the Deepline CLI.
- [gtm-skills (gtm-skills/gtm)](https://github.com/gtm-skills/gtm) - a GTM toolkit for AI agents covering research, intel, outreach, and CRM.
- [gtm-skills (LeadMagic)](https://github.com/LeadMagic/gtm-skills) - 205 production GTM agent skills for Claude Code spanning sales, outbound, ABM, PLG, and customer success, each cited to a named framework.
- [marketingagentskills](https://github.com/realjaymes/marketingagentskills) - 29 AI agent skills for marketing tasks; adjacent to GTM engineering rather than GTM-specific.

## Data & Enrichment

Well-known providers in the GTM/RevOps data stack. Inclusion here is not an
endorsement, just a map of what's out there.

- [Apollo.io](https://www.apollo.io) - combined B2B contact database and sales engagement platform.
- [Clay](https://clay.com) - the enrichment/orchestration tool that popularized the term "GTM engineer."
- [Cognism](https://www.cognism.com) - B2B contact data with a GDPR-focused EMEA data set.
- [HubSpot Breeze Intelligence](https://clearbit.com/) - formerly Clearbit; HubSpot acquired it and folded it into HubSpot as a Breeze Intelligence add-on, so it's now effectively HubSpot-only.
- [People Data Labs](https://www.peopledatalabs.com) - person and company data infrastructure provider used inside many enrichment waterfalls.
- [RB2B](https://www.rb2b.com) - identifies anonymous US website visitors at the person level and pushes profiles to Slack.
- [Warmly](https://www.warmly.ai) - broader visitor-identification and revenue-orchestration platform, company and person-level, with automated outbound plays.
