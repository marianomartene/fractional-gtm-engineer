Awesome Fractional GTM

A curated guide to fractional go-to-market: the operators, tools, playbooks, and frameworks behind hiring a part-time GTM leader or GTM engineer instead of a full-time team.

Fractional GTM is how early-stage and lean B2B companies get senior go-to-market firepower without a full-time hire: a part-time operator or GTM engineer who builds the pipeline system, then hands it off or keeps running it. This list maps the space: what the role is, who does it (peers and competitors included, listed fairly), the tooling, and the playbooks worth reading. Every entry below is a real, verifiable page. Nothing here is invented.

Maintained by Mariano Martene, a fractional GTM engineer. Source and contribution guide: the repo.
